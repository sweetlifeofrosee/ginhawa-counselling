About Ginhawa

A Practice Founded on Compassion, Culture, and Care

Ginhawa Counselling Services was founded by a Filipina counsellor living in Canada — rooted in the belief that every person deserves to heal in a space where they are truly understood.

Our Story

Founded With a Filipino Heart, For Everyone Who Needs to Heal

Ginhawa Counselling Services was founded by a Filipina counsellor who came to Canada carrying both the richness of her cultural heritage and the lived experience of navigating a new country — its opportunities, its challenges, and the quiet weight of being far from home.

In Filipino culture, there is a profound value placed on kapwa — a sense of shared humanity, of seeing yourself in others. That same spirit lives in how we practice: with deep respect for each person's story, patience with the pace of healing, and a genuine belief in the strength that already exists within every client who walks through our door.

The name Ginhawa was chosen with intention. It means relief, comfort, and peace of mind — the feeling of a long breath finally released. That is what we hope every client carries with them after each session.

"You don't have to have it all figured out to begin. Healing starts with one honest conversation."

Our Philosophy

Evidence-Based. Heart-Led. Always Client-Centered.

We integrate proven therapeutic methods within a relationship that is genuine, respectful, and attuned to your individual needs. No single approach works for everyone — which is why we tailor everything to your unique story and goals.

We draw on a range of evidence-based modalities, always in service of what you need — not a predetermined program.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT) Trauma-Informed Care Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT) Mindfulness-Based Approaches Narrative Therapy Solution-Focused Therapy Culturally Sensitive Practice
