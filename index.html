Ginhawa

A Filipino word that carries the feeling of relief, comfort, ease, and peace of mind — the quiet exhale after tension, the lightness that comes from being truly heard. Our practice is named Ginhawa because that is exactly what we hold for every client: a space where healing begins.

Why Ginhawa

A Safe Space Built on Trust, Compassion, and Cultural Understanding

We believe every person deserves to feel genuinely heard — without judgment, without pressure, and at their own pace.

Compassionate, Client-Centered Care

Your goals, your pace, your story — we follow your lead with warmth and genuine curiosity.

Confidential & Secure

Everything you share stays private, protected by professional ethical standards and Canadian privacy law.

Evidence-Based Approaches

We draw on CBT, ACT, trauma-informed care, and mindfulness — adapted to fit you as an individual.

Culturally Inclusive Care

We honour your background and identity. Culturally informed practice — especially for Filipino-Canadians — is at our core.

Online & In-Person Sessions

Flexible formats to fit your life — secure video sessions or in-person appointments available across Canada.
